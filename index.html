FAQ

India travel ke common questions ka simple answer.

India travel ka best time?

Most routes ke liye October to March comfortable hota hai. Mountains ke liye season alag ho sakta hai.

First trip ke liye best route?

Delhi, Agra, Jaipur beginner-friendly route hai. Culture aur heritage dono milte hain.

Budget kaise plan kare?

Stay, food, transport, entry tickets aur local travel ko separate calculate karo.
