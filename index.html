States of India

Every state has its own language, tradition, dress, food, music and story.

Rajasthan

Land of royal forts, deserts, colorful turbans, folk music and grand palaces.

Kerala

Known for backwaters, coconut trees, Ayurveda, Kathakali and peaceful nature.

Punjab

Full of energy, farming culture, bhangra, Golden Temple and warm hospitality.

Uttarakhand

A beautiful Himalayan state with temples, rivers, mountains and spiritual places.

🎉

Festivals

Diwali, Holi, Eid, Christmas, Baisakhi, Onam and many more festivals bring people together.

Dance & Music

From Bharatanatyam to Bhangra, Indian art forms show emotion, devotion and celebration.

Traditions

Food of India

Indian food is famous for spices, flavors, colors and variety from every region.

Biryani

Aromatic rice dish with spices, herbs and rich regional styles.

Dosa

South India's crispy classic served with chutney and sambar.

Samosa

A popular street snack filled with spicy potatoes and served hot.

Indian Thali

A complete meal with roti, rice, dal, sabzi, pickle, sweet and more.

Famous Places

India has mountains, beaches, temples, forts, forests, cities and peaceful villages.

Taj Mahal

A world-famous symbol of love and one of India's most iconic monuments.

Varanasi

One of the world's oldest living cities, known for ghats, Ganga Aarti and spirituality.

Goa

Beautiful beaches, nightlife, seafood, churches and relaxed coastal vibes.

Himalayas

Snowy mountains, peaceful valleys, adventure treks and spiritual destinations.
